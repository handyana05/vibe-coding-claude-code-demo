§ 03 — Manifesto

A school, not a bootcamp.

We believe artificial intelligence deserves the patience of a serious education, not the speed of a productivity hack. Our students read papers, derive equations, train small models from first principles, and argue about consequences. They graduate with intuition — the rarest and most expensive thing in this field — and with work they can point to.

— The faculty
